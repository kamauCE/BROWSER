Tor Browser

Characteristics of Tor Browser

  • Anonymity by routing internet traffic making it difficult to be tracked.
  • Encryption ensuring data is secure and not easily accessible by hackers.
  • Access to Blocked websites and online services that may be blocked or restricted.
  • Multi-Layered security to protect online privacy.
  • No History or cookies as they are automatically deleted after each session.
  • Easy setup as it comes with a user friendly interface
  • Community support as it has an active community of developers and users.
